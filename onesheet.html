About

3Steez (Trés Styles) is a Southeast Michigan DJ, MC, and event producer with a decade-plus track record of commanding rooms from 150 to 1,200 people. For 4 years as the resident MC/Host of Frequency Saturdays at The Necto — alongside resident DJ Skoob E (the main resident DJ) — Trés helped build and curate what became the largest weekly party in Southeast Michigan, drawing 750 to 1,200 people every Saturday, with lines down the block on packed nights. He played a key role in reshaping the night into a premier ladies' night and driving the event's comeback post-Covid. During that run, The Necto was nominated for Best Nightclub in Washtenaw County three years in a row, DJ Skoob E won Best DJ three consecutive years, and Trés earned a Best MC nomination each year as the event's only resident MC.

Now active across multiple Detroit-area rooms, Trés currently holds residencies at Alley Bar (Wednesdays), Rabbit Hole (Fridays), and a monthly at Live — spinning R&B, Hip-Hop, and Soul sets that read the crowd and keep the floor locked from open to close.
