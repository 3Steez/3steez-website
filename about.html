Biography

The Story Behind the Sound

3Steez (Trés Styles) is a Southeast Michigan DJ, MC, and event producer with a decade-plus track record of commanding rooms from 150 to 1,200 people. Michigan-born and Detroit-raised, Trés has spent over two decades mastering the craft of reading a room, building energy from zero, and keeping a crowd locked in from open to close.

He’s not just a DJ who plays records or an MC who talks between songs. He’s an event architect — someone who understands the arc of a night and executes it with precision. Whether he’s spinning R&B, Hip-Hop, and Soul at a nightclub residency or commanding a corporate stage in front of 500 executives, 3Steez brings the same concert-level energy and boardroom-level professionalism.

Frequency Saturdays @ The Necto — 4 Year Residency

For 4 years as the resident MC/Host of Frequency Saturdays at The Necto alongside resident DJ Skoob E (the main resident DJ), Trés helped build and curate what became the largest weekly party in Southeast Michigan, drawing 750 to 1,200 people every Saturday.

During that run, The Necto was nominated for Best Nightclub three years in a row, DJ Skoob E won Best DJ three consecutive years, and Trés earned a Best MC nomination each year as the event’s only resident MC.

Now active across multiple Detroit-area rooms, Trés currently holds residencies at Alley Bar (Wednesdays), Rabbit Hole (Fridays), and a monthly at Live Nightclub.
