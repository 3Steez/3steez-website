Biography

Trés Styles
aka 3Steez

“Trés Steez is the vibe your event is missing.”

Trés Styles isn’t a performer you hire — he’s an experience you invest in. A Michigan-born MC, DJ, and music producer with over two decades in the game, he has built a reputation that speaks before he ever picks up a mic: commanding, versatile, and relentlessly professional.

From running the room at Detroit’s most electric club nights to sharing stages with hip-hop royalty — ?uestlove, Stretch Armstrong, Kool DJ Red Alert — to producing beats that have shaped countless artists’ careers, Trés brings a depth of craft and cultural fluency that no ordinary entertainment service can replicate.

For corporate clients and festival buyers, 3Steez represents something rare: concert-level energy delivered with boardroom-level reliability. Every engagement is custom, every performance is intentional, and every crowd leaves satisfied.

As founder of Forekast Music, LLC, Trés has built more than a performance career — he’s built an entertainment infrastructure that supports artists, brands, and event producers looking for the highest level of creative and professional partnership.

The Corporate Partnership
Behind the Brand

Trés Styles doesn’t just perform at corporate events — he helps engineer them. As DJ Graffiti’s agent, manager, and sales partner, Trés bridges the gap between elite DJ talent and the corporate clients who need it most — handling bookings, event strategy, and performance logistics for one of the most respected names in the DJ world.

Together, they’ve built a turnkey corporate entertainment solution that delivers at the highest level, every time.
